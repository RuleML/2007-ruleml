We are pleased to offer the opportunity to be a sponsor of the RuleML-2007 Symposium to be held in Orlando, Florida, October 25-26, 2007.

Co-located with the 10th International Business Rules Forum, the International Symposium on Rule Interchange and Applications (RuleML-2007) is the first symposium devoted to work on practical distributed rule and event-processing technologies and rule-based applications which need language standards for rules operating in the context of, e.g., the Semantic Web, Web 2.0/Web 3.0, Intelligent Multi-Agent Systems, Enterprise Service Component Architectures, Event-Driven Architectures and Service-Oriented Computing Applications. After a series of successful RuleML workshops and then conferences (past events), this is a  new kind of event where the Web Logic community joins the established, practically oriented Forum of the Business Rules community to help cross-fertilizing between Web and Business Logic technology.

RuleML-2007 provides an excellent opportunity for your organization to be associated with this innovation driven technology market, characterized by strong market growth, visibility and commercial opportunities.
